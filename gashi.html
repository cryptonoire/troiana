Overview

Gashi Garten und Tiefbau handles everything around gardens and outdoor spaces — from design and planting to maintenance and groundworks (Tiefbau). The goal was a professional online presence that communicates reliability and care, and makes it simple for customers to request work.

We built a clean, fast, mobile-first site that presents the services clearly and drives enquiries.
