Overview

Elshani Garten designs and builds outdoor spaces — "green oases" tailored to each client. The business needed a professional, modern website that conveyed quality craftsmanship and made it easy for homeowners in the Gilching area to understand the services and get in touch.

We delivered a fast, mobile-first marketing site that puts the work front and centre and funnels visitors toward a quick contact.

What we built

Service showcase

Clear presentation of core services — paving, terraces, wooden decks, fences, lawn/sod installation and winter maintenance.

Trust-building design

A clean, natural aesthetic that signals reliability and quality craftsmanship to local homeowners.

Easy contact

Phone and email calls-to-action throughout so enquiries are only a tap away on mobile.

Local SEO foundations

Fast, semantic, mobile-first build to help the company surface for garden & landscaping searches around Gilching.
