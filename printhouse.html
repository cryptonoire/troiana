Overview

PrintHouse is a print, signage and promotional-products business that needed a professional online presence to match the quality of its work. The brief: a clean, trustworthy marketing site that doubles as a browsable product catalogue, so prospects can explore offerings and request quotes before ever walking through the door.

We designed and built the entire site end to end — brand-aligned visual design, a structured product catalogue, and a snappy, mobile-first front end built for speed and easy updates.

The challenge

Printing companies live and die by trust and clarity — customers need to immediately understand the range of services (business cards, large-format signage, banners, promotional merchandise) and feel confident in the quality. The previous setup relied on phone calls and word of mouth, with no way to showcase the catalogue or capture enquiries online.

The site had to load instantly, look credible on every device, and make requesting a quote effortless.

What we built

Brand-led marketing site

A confident, clean design in PrintHouse's signature blue and white, built to communicate quality and reliability at a glance.

Product catalogue

Structured categories for printing, signage and promotional products so visitors can browse the full range and understand what's on offer.

Quote & enquiry flow

Clear calls-to-action throughout that funnel visitors toward requesting a quote — turning browsers into leads.

Mobile-first & fast

Built on Vite + React for instant loads and a smooth experience on phones, where most local customers find the business.

Easy to maintain

A component-driven Tailwind codebase that's simple to extend with new products, services and pages as the business grows.

SEO foundations

Semantic markup, fast Core Web Vitals and a clear structure to help local customers find PrintHouse on search.
